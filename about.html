ABOUT US

Welcome to our website dedicated to providing basic knowledge about objects in space, crafted with precision and creativity. This project, submitted for the NJACK SUMMER OF CODE (NSOC), is a collaborative effort by three enthusiastic students from the Indian Institute of Technology, Patna.

Our team consists of Mihika, and Kshitij. Mihika, a second-year BTech student in Computer Science and Engineering, brings her expertise in coding and her innovative approach to web design. Her technical acumen and attention to detail ensure that our website is not only informative but also user-friendly and visually stunning.

Kshitij, also in his second year, is pursuing BTech in Mechanical Engineering. His keen understanding of engineering principles and his passion for space science have been instrumental in creating content that is both accurate and engaging. Kshitij's ability to simplify complex concepts makes our website an excellent resource for anyone interested in learning about space.

Together, we have combined our skills to create a platform that uses extensive animations to make learning about space objects both fun and educational. We hope our efforts inspire curiosity and a love for space in all our visitors. Thank you for exploring with us!
